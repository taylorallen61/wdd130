Home page

Main information Second Helpings Thrift Store is located at 16 E Main St in Rexburg, Idaho at the Family Crisis Center! When you walk in the store, the store is located down the stairs. All proceeds from our store go directly to helping clients of the Family Crisis Center, who are survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault. Our clients also have the privilege of shopping from the store for free when physical needs arise. It does this by raising money through the purchases made in the thrift store, all proceeds go to helping these victims. It also provides those victims with their physical needs, such as clothing, bedding, food, and other essential items. Hours of operation The thrift store is open Monday-Saturday from 10am - 4:30pm, Closed on all National holidays.

Get Involved

How to Donate The drop off location is located behind the building in the florences chocolate parking lot. There is a large painted mural where you will find a doorbell stating “Ring for donations.” It is preferred you donate during hours of operation or else you will have to leave your items outside until the store is opened the next day. If you ring anytime during the store hours, an assistant will collect your donations. We accept any and all donations, but are most in need of food and clothing. Large items are difficult for us to fit in our store, so we recommend donating those items to Deseret Industries. Volunteer opportunities Second Helpings thrift store welcomes and is seeking help from volunteers. Shifts can be anytime in operational hours. Shifts can be as long as 1 - 6 hours and as often as youd like. If you would like to sign up this is the link. https://app.initlive.com/web-admin/app/#/join-party?k=nfqcwle1tjmqyn Our volunteers do a number of things including, pricing and putting away clothes, organizing and rearranging the store, cleaning and vacuuming the store, and assisting clients in their shopping needs.

FAQ

F A Q Why should I shop at Second Helpings? Second Helpings is a non-profit organization and has a mission to help those in need. All proceeds go to victims of sexual assault, abuse, and domestic violence. When you shop at second helpings you are able to have a fun shopping experience while also helping those in your community who are struggling. Can I receive free items? Not all members of the community get free items. If you are a victim of sexual assault, abuse or domestic violence, the family crises is here to help you. You are able to meet with our victim advocates who are able to give you resources to help you. One of those resources is providing you with any physical needs you may need which can be found in the thrift store. Where does the proceeds of sales go? One-hundred percent of proceeds go straight to victims of sexual assault, abuse, domestic violence and stalking. How do I donate? You are able to donate during any of the operational hours, 10-4:30 mondays-saturdays. If you go to the back of the store you will be able to see a big painted mural. On the left side of the mural you will see a doorbell that says “ring for donations” when you ring that doorbell you will be assisted and your donations will be collected. We appreciate all donations as they truly do help community members in need. What items can I donate? We accept all food and clothing items. Our food will be given away during our foodbank on Wednesdays and Fridays. We discourage donating large items such as bikes or furniture as our thrift store is small and there isn’t too much room for those items.
