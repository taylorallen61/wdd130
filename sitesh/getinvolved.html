How to Get Involved

How to Donate?

Instructions

The drop off location is located behind the building in the florences chocolate parking lot. There is a large painted mural where you will find a doorbell stating “Ring for donations.”

It is preferred you donate during hours of operation or else you will have to leave your items outside until the store is opened the next day. If you ring anytime during the store hours, an assistant will collect your donations.

We accept any and all donations, but are most in need of food and clothing. Large items are difficult for us to fit in our store, so we recommend donating those items to Deseret Industries.

Volunteer opportunities Second Helpings thrift store welcomes and is seeking help from volunteers. Shifts can be anytime in operational hours. Shifts can be as long as 1 - 6 hours and as often as youd like. If you would like to sign up this is the link.
